Nestled in the heart of Nepal lies Kathmandu, a city steeped in history and adorned with vibrant culture. From the sacred temples of Swayambhunath to the bustling streets of Thamel, Kathmandu offers a sensory feast for travelers. Explore the intricate woodcarvings of Kathmandu Durbar Square or lose yourself in the aromatic alleys of Asan Bazaar. In Kathmandu, every corner tells a story, making it a must-visit destination for culture enthusiasts.

Lalitpur, also known as Patan, is a hidden gem in the Kathmandu Valley. Renowned for its exquisite Newari architecture and ancient temples, Lalitpur transports visitors to a bygone era. Wander through the narrow streets of Mangal Bazaar, where artisans craft intricate metalwork and pottery. Marvel at the ornate beauty of Patan Durbar Square, a UNESCO World Heritage Site adorned with stunning palaces and temples. Lalitpur promises an enchanting journey through Nepal's rich cultural heritage.

Step into the past with a visit to Bhaktapur, an ancient city renowned for its well-preserved heritage and traditional craftsmanship. Meander through the car-free lanes of Bhaktapur Durbar Square, where majestic temples and royal palaces stand as testament to the city's illustrious history. Admire the intricate woodcarvings of Nyatapola Temple or witness local artisans at work in the pottery square. Bhaktapur's timeless beauty and cultural significance make it a captivating destination for travelers seeking authenticity.
